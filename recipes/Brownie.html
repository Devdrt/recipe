Cast Iron Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Butter melted
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup flower

    • Now that you have the ingredients lets start making the brownies!

    1. Preheat oven to 350 with a 12 inch cast iron skillet
    2. Melt butter in a large bowl whish together sugar,coca powder and salt. pour in butter and stir
    3. add the eggs and vanilla extrtact, whisk again add the flour and whisk, Do not over mix butter
    4. Pour the batter into the skillet and place back in for 30 mins, they will not be fully cooked yet let sit in pan for an hour
